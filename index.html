How it works

Up and running in 3 steps

1
⭳

Install VikingClaw

One command installs the binary. Works on Windows, Mac, Linux, and Raspberry Pi.

irm vikingclaw.com/install.ps1 | iex
2
🧠

Load a Local Model

Open LM Studio or Ollama, download any model. VikingClaw auto-detects it instantly — no config.

ollama pull qwen2.5:7b
3
⚡

Open Your Dashboard

Run vikingclaw start and open localhost:7070. Your private AI HQ is live.

vikingclaw start → localhost:7070

Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

A complete AI platform that respects your privacy and works on hardware you already own.

💬

AI Chat

Stream word-by-word responses from any local model. Tool calls, memory, reasoning — all local.

🧠

Smart Model Manager

Auto-detects your hardware. Recommends what models fit your PC. One-click download with live progress.

🔐

Security Sandbox

5-layer sandbox, credential scrubbing, tamper-evident audit log. Know exactly what your AI can touch.

🌐

Built-in Browser

Headless browser your AI controls. Search, scrape, automate — without a cloud middleman.

⚡

Automation Engine

Visual rule builder. Cron jobs, triggers, multi-step chains. Schedule tasks to run while you sleep.

📈

Live Mind Map

Interactive graph of your entire AI system — models, tools, memory, automations — all connected.

📋

Google Workspace

Connect Gmail, Drive, Calendar, Docs. Your AI reads and acts on your Google data — entirely locally.

🤖

Telegram Bot

Talk to your agent from anywhere via Telegram. It reads files, runs code, and searches memory remotely.

🏠

Runs Anywhere

Single Go binary. Raspberry Pi, old laptop, VPS. Under 20MB RAM idle. Under 1 second to start.
